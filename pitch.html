Chapter 06 · The Stack Voice · Dascha (cloned)
Production stack — Sunday delivery

Which tools we use
for the 2-minute film.

Two questions: what video tool, and which image tools does Claude provide? Honest answer below.

Video · Primary
Our pick

CapCut Desktop (Pro) + Descript

  • CapCut Desktop Pro — edits our handheld footage, auto-captions in EN/JA/DE, zero learning curve, free tier covers the full 2-minute deliverable. Keyframe animation is good enough for the AnswerAtlas matrix reveal.
  • Descript — "text-based" video editing. We paste the script, it edits the video to match. Critical for Sunday-morning re-cuts after jury feedback.
  • ElevenLabs (already in stack) delivers the voice clones. We drop the JA/DE audio into CapCut as separate tracks.
  • Why not Runway / Sora? — the footage that matters is our real faces on our couch. AI-generated video would undercut the founder-market-fit story.
Video · Backup AI
Only if needed

Runway Gen-4 · Kling 2.0 · Sora

  • Runway Gen-4 — best for the Tokyo b-roll if no real couple available. 10-second clips, strong low-light.
  • Kling 2.0 — best quality for Asian faces in dim lighting (the Tokyo scene). Free tier generous enough for hackathon.
  • Use these only for the Tokyo cutaway (0:55–1:15). Everything else stays real footage.
Images · Via Claude
Honest answer

Claude does not generate images natively

  • Claude cannot produce images — it can read and analyze images you paste in, and it can write prompts, HTML/SVG, or Mermaid diagrams that render visually. That is what built this page.
  • Best image tools to pair with Claude: Nano Banana 2 (Google Gemini 3 image) for the Tokyo couple shot — fastest, best Asian-face fidelity in April 2026. Seedream 4.0 for editorial warmth. Midjourney v7 for the cinematic product beats.
  • Workflow: ask Claude for the prompt → paste into Nano Banana 2 → bring the render back to Claude to critique or revise the prompt. This is the pitch-doc sourcing chain already.
  • For the AnswerAtlas dashboard visuals: we build directly in Lovable (already in the partner stack) — screen-record the actual dashboard. No AI image needed.
Voice · Differentiator
The craft move

ElevenLabs Voice Clone + Multilingual v2

  • Clone Matthi's and Dascha's real voices Saturday evening (3 min of clean audio each).
  • Generate the JA line — 「朝、私たちは十五分、一緒に息をします」 — in Matthi's actual voice. Every other team does English TTS. We speak the mother tongue of the couple we're reaching.
  • Same clones power the app itself post-launch. Production doubles as product asset.
