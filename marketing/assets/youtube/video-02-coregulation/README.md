## How to use

Scratch narration to pace and time your live takes. Cards drop in for chapter breaks — YouTube's chapter feature will pick them up automatically once you set timestamps in the description.

Cards are SVG (1920×1080). Rasterize via `rsvg-convert` or Chrome screenshot.
